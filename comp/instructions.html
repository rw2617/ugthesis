Instructions

In this study, you will be paired randomly and anonymously in groups of 3 for 15 rounds. Two of you will be the Sellers and the other will be the Buyer. Your role is randomly assigned and constant throughout the session.

Buyers will have a value of either 50 or 150 experimental units, which is randomly assigned every round. The Sellers will each offer a price, and the Buyer can either accept or reject each price. If the Buyer accepts a price, the Seller who offered this price will receive the proposed offer and the Buyer will receive 50 - price or 150 - price. If the Buyer rejects all offers, all participants will receive 0.

The Buyer has a value of 50 points with probability 50% and a value of 150 points with probability 50%.
