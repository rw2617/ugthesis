Instructions

In this study, you will be paired randomly and anonymously with two other participants for x rounds. Two of you will be the sellers and the other will be the buyer. Your role is randomly assigned and constant throughout the session.

While all sellers have the same cost of 0 experimental units, buyers will have a value of either 50 or 150 experimental units, which is randomly assigned every round. The seller will offer a price, and the buyer can either accept or reject this price. If the buyer accepts a price, the seller who offered this price will receive the proposed offer and the buyer will receive either 50 - price or 150 - price. If the buyer rejects all offers, all participants will receive 0.
