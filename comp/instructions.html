Instructions

In this study, you will be paired randomly and anonymously in groups of three for 15 rounds. Two of you will be the Sellers and the other will be the Buyer. Your role is randomly assigned and constant throughout the session.

Buyers will have a value of either 50 or 150 points, which is randomly assigned every round. The Sellers will each offer a price, and the Buyer can accept at most one price. If the Buyer accepts a price, the Seller who offered this price will receive the proposed offer and the Buyer will receive 50 minus price or 150 minus price. The other Seller will receive 0 points. If the Buyer rejects all offers, all participants in the group will receive 0 points for the round.

The Buyer has a value of 50 points with probability 50% and a value of 150 points with probability 50%.
