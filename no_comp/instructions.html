Instructions

In this study, you will be paired randomly and anonymously in groups of 2 for 15 rounds. One of you will be the seller and the other will be the buyer. This role is randomly assigned and constant throughout the session.

Buyers will have a value of either 50 or 150 experimental units, which is randomly assigned every round. The seller will offer a price, and the buyer can either accept or reject this price. If the buyer accepts a price, the seller who offered this price will receive the proposed offer and the buyer will receive 50 - price or 150 - price. If the buyer rejects all offers, all participants will receive 0.

The buyer has a value of 50 points with probability 50% and a value of 150 points with probability 50%.
